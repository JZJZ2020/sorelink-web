F5G Solutions

POL — Passive Optical LAN

Built on GPON/XGS-PON technology, with OLT as the core, passive optical splitters as relays, and ONUs as endpoints — constructing a fully passive all-optical network. Compared to traditional copper cabling, POL saves 90% telecom room space, reduces 30% energy consumption, and supports 20 years of maintenance-free operation.

FTTO fiber-to-classroom, supporting Education Digitalization 2.0. One fiber covering teaching buildings, dormitories, and labs for 10G campus networks.
