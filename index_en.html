SORLINK——Pioneer in the core research and development of all-optical network intelligence

SORLINK was founded in 2015 and located in Shanghai, China. Our company is dedicated to R&D, production, sales and service for GPON ONU, xDSL DSLAM, EOC,PLC, Switch.

SORLINK Ltd offers tailored wired line networking products for various deployment requirements in the communication industry. We can provide customizable system board and line card.

SORLINK has professional R&D team,Our engineers have at least 10 years of experience in working with telecom devices, technology and tools. In addition to our own competence, we have an extensive contact network of research and development experts in different technology areas. We also co-operate closely with the research community in areas that support us and our customers.
