Product Overview

The SORELINK S2901Q-A is a next-generation 10G PON terminal device designed to provide an extreme network access experience.

Equipped with a 2.5Gbps network port, it perfectly unleashes the performance potential of 10G PON. It supports both routing and bridging modes and is compatible with mainstream XG-PON and XGS-PON networks, making it the ideal choice for gigabit-plus broadband plans.

  • Support for XG-PON and XGS-PON
  • 1 * 2.5G Port, backward compatible with 1G/100M
  • IPv4/IPv6 Dual Stack Support
  • Compact Design with Efficient Cooling

Key Advantages

2.5G Extreme Port

Breaks the tradition gigabit bottleneck, providing a multi-gigabit data path for high-end Wi-Fi 6/7 routers or NAS.

Broad Compatibility

Fully compliant with ITU-T G.9807 (XGS-PON) and G.987 (XG-PON) standards, ensuring interoperability with major OLT brands.

Secure & Dependable

Built-in firewall and encryption mechanisms to protect user data privacy and integrity during transmission.
