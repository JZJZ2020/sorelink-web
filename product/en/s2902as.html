Product Overview

The SORELINK S2902A-S is a high-performance XG-PON/XGS-PON HGU device designed to meet the growing digital needs of modern homes and small offices.

In addition to standard gigabit Ethernet ports, it features an SFP slot, allowing users to flexibly configure optical-to-electrical modules or leased-line access according to actual needs. Supporting L3 routing protocols and VoIP voice functions, it is a comprehensive access terminal that integrates high-speed internet and voice communication.
