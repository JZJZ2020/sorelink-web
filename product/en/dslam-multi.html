Product Overview

SORELINK DSLAM series products are designed to help operators and enterprises utilize existing copper infrastructure to deploy broadband services quickly and efficiently.

Supporting the latest VDSL2 and ADSL2+ standards, it provides excellent coverage while ensuring high bandwidth. With powerful management functions and physical isolation security, it is an ideal choice for broadband coverage in existing urban communities, remote areas, and old campus network upgrades.
