Product Overview

The SORELINK Wi-Fi 5 ONT is a high-performance, cost-effective XPON access terminal. It supports simultaneous 2.4GHz and 5GHz dual-band wireless connection with a total bandwidth of up to 1200Mbps.

This device not only provides 2 gigabit Ethernet ports for wired connections but also fully supports the EasyMesh standard, allowing it to form a home Mesh network with other roaming devices of the same brand. It is an ideal choice for residential broadband popularization and FTTH upgrades, providing a stable and fast online environment.
