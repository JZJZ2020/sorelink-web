Product Overview

The SORELINK Wi-Fi 6 HGU ONT is a flagship smart gateway designed for high-end home users and SOHO environments. It integrates adaptive XPON access, high-performance routing, and dual-band Wi-Fi 6 wireless networking.

Supporting 160MHz bandwidth and MU-MIMO technology, it ensures multiple devices remain smoothly connected simultaneously. The built-in USB 3.0 port supports local file sharing and print server functions. Whether it's 8K video streaming, online gaming, or large-scale smart home integration, it provides rock-solid connectivity.
