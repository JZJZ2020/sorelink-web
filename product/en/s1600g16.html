Product Overview

The SORELINK S1600G16 is a high-density, carrier-class GPON OLT designed for medium-scale access requirements.

It integrates L3 switching functions and supports full-service access for data, voice, and IP video. Within a 1U compact chassis, it provides 16 GPON ports, capable of supporting over 2000 ONUs, making it a cost-effective choice for FTTB/FTTH deployments.

  • 16 GPON Ports, supports up to 2048 ONUs
  • 4 * 10GE SFP+ Uplinks + 4 * GE Uplinks
  • Redundant Power Support (AC+DC)
  • Hot-swappable PON Modules

Key Advantages

L3 Switching Architecture

Built-in high-performance L3 wire-speed switching engine supporting various routing protocols for complex networking.

High Reliability Redundancy

Dual power supply and uplink aggregation ensure 7x24h uninterrupted operation for core communication nodes.

Unified Management

Centralized management via SNMP, CLI, or EMS for zero-config deployment and improved maintenance efficiency.
