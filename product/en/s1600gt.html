Product Overview

The SORELINK S1600GT is a compact, low-density GPON/XG(S)-PON OLT designed to meet the flexible needs of modern enterprises, hotels, and small campuses for all-optical networks.

Its highly integrated design allows installation in a standard 1U rack or smaller spaces. With support for up to 2 PON ports and a 1:128 split ratio per port, it is an ideal choice for building a high-speed digital foundation.

  • Supports GPON/XG-PON/XGS-PON Multi-mode
  • 208Gbps System Backplane Capacity
  • High-density Uplinks: 10GE SFP+
  • Low Power Consumption / Fanless Design

Key Advantages

Extreme Bandwidth

Support for 10G PON evolution, providing symmetric 10Gbps bandwidth for 4K/8K video and cloud services.

Carrier-Class Security

Comprehensive security management protocols to prevent illegal access and protect enterprise data.

Simple Architecture

Optimized for POL (Passive Optical LAN), simplifying network layers and reducing TCO significantly.
