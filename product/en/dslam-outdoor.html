Product Overview

The SORELINK Outdoor Mini DSLAM MDU is an access device specifically designed for fiber-to-the-curb/building (FTTC/FTTB) scenarios.

Featuring an IP67 fully sealed aluminum alloy housing, it provides excellent thermal performance and electromagnetic compatibility. Supporting stable operation in extreme temperatures from -40°C to +65°C, it allows direct deployment on utility poles, exterior walls, or greenbelts without independent air-conditioned cabinets. It is a powerful tool for operators to improve last-mile coverage quality.
