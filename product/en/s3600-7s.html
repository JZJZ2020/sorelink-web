Product Overview

The SORELINK S3600-7S is a core-level device for optical access networks, tailored for ultra-large user access and high-throughput business scenarios.

It features 7 universal service slots, providing up to 112 GPON or 56 XGS-PON connections. With its non-blocking switching architecture, it offers unparalleled data forwarding capabilities, serving as the backbone for carrier-grade operations and large-scale all-optical network architectures.

  • 7 Service Slots, Massive Scalability
  • Up to 112 PON Port Capacity
  • Core-level 14Tbps Switching Bandwidth
  • Full Redundancy for Control, Power, and Fans
