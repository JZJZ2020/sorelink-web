Product Overview

The SORELINK S3600-2S is a modular chassis OLT designed with advanced distributed switching architecture.

It provides 2 universal service slots, supporting hybrid insertion of GPON, XG-PON, and XGS-PON line cards. Its compact 2U chassis design not only saves rack space but also provides carrier-grade reliability through fully redundant control and power modules.

  • 2 Hybrid Service Slots
  • Maximum 32 PON Ports
  • 100GE Uplink Support
  • 1+1 Redundant Control & Power

Specifications

Dimensions Standard 19-inch 2U Height
Slot Count 2*Service slots, 2*MCU slots, 2*Power slots
Max Capacity 32*GPON / 16*XGS-PON Ports
Uplink Interface Up to 100GE/40GE/10GE via Control Units
Switching Capacity 3.2 Tbps (Distributed)
