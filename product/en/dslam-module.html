Product Overview

The SORELINK 8-port Outdoor DSLAM Module is an extremely compact broadband access terminal solution.

It is primarily aimed at access in very low-density villages or scattered residential areas. Its hallmark feature is high integration, with built-in lightning protection, power management, and high-performance VDSL2 forwarding logic. The device supports remote power supply through twisted pairs, significantly simplifying site selection and construction. Even on poles or walls without local power, it can provide reliable gigabit-level drop services.
