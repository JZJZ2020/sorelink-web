Product Overview

The SORELINK S283D-4G is a 4-port XPON SFU device designed specifically for high-reliability access scenarios. It features a rugged metal casing that not only enhances physical protection but also ensures long-term stable operation in enclosed environments through passive cooling.

Providing 4 gigabit Ethernet ports and supporting VLAN and QoS policies, it is ideal for use in industrial cabinets, outdoor enclosures, or commercial buildings where stability is paramount. It is a prime example of wide-temperature operation and anti-interference design, capable of meeting various complex network deployment challenges.
