Product Overview

The SORELINK S2801Q is a minimalist, high-performance XPON SFU terminal. It can automatically identify and switch between GPON or EPON network modes, greatly reducing deployment complexity and inventory pressure for operators.

Equipped with a single 2.5GbE network port, it easily supports service flows above gigabit broadband, providing ample data throughput for users' PCs or high-performance routers. It is a highly cost-effective entry-level 2.5G PON terminal solution.
