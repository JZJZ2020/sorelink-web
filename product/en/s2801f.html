Product Overview

The SORELINK S2801F is an innovative SFP-encapsulated XPON ONT. It breaks the limitations of traditional ONTs needing separate power and extra space by adopting standard SFP interface protocols.

This product can be plugged directly into SFP-supporting switches, routers, or wireless APs, instantly granting them PON network access capabilities. Supporting GPON and EPON adaptive modes, it's perfect for minimalist network equipment integration and specialized vertical industry applications needing PON access.
