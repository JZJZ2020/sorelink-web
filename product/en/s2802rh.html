Product Overview

The SORELINK S2802RH is a dual-mode XPON access terminal deeply optimized for enterprise all-optical local area networks (POL) and fiber-to-the-office (FTTO) scenarios.

It provides 1 * 2.5GbE and 1 * 1GbE user interfaces, meeting the high-speed access requirements of core office equipment and secondary device expansion simultaneously. With its compact and durable housing design and flexible installation options, it is the ideal partner for all-optical network deployment in government, education, healthcare, and modern enterprise campuses.
