Product Overview

The SORELINK S3600G1-C is a flagship 10G PON access device designed to meet the growing demand for high-bandwidth services.

It integrates industry-leading Combo PON technology, allowing GPON and XG(S)-PON services to be provided over the same fiber simultaneously, enabling smooth network upgrades. Equipped with high-bandwidth uplink interfaces, it meets the requirements of ultra-broadband campuses and industry cloud office needs.

  • 8 XG(S)-PON & GPON Combo Ports
  • 100Gbps QSFP28 Uplink Support
  • 1960Gbps Total Switching Capacity
  • Robust Carrier-Grade Power Protection

Key Advantages

Combo PON Technology

Seamless migration from GPON to 10G PON without replacing existing splitters or ODN networks.

Ultra Uplink Bandwidth

Equipped with 100G uplink ports, effectively eliminating network bottlenecks for massive data concurrency.

Cloud-Ready Architecture

Fully supports virtualization and SDN management, adapting to flexible cloud-based application architectures.
