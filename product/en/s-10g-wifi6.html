Product Overview

The SORELINK 10G Wi-Fi 6 HGU is our highest-performance consumer access device to date. It not only supports ultra-high-speed fiber access via XGS-PON but also ensures that ultra-gigabit traffic can be losslessly transmitted to downstream devices through its 2.5GE network port.

The device adopts the latest AX3000 wireless standard, with hardware-level optimization for high-density, low-latency requirements. It integrates triple-play forwarding logic for voice, video, and high-speed data, serving as the core hub for whole-home fiber (FTTR) and high-end smart home projects.
